AdzeKit™

Prehistoric tools, modern brains, future-proof productivity

AdzeKit™ is a markdown-native cognitive scaffold for an AI-disrupted future.

It isn't a productivity system — it's a cognitive scaffold meant to keep humans in control of their AI use. It's damn simple. It extends the Bullet Journal philosophy into the AI world.

A prehistoric toolbox for future-proof productivity: a Bullet Journal–compatible workbench where you offload your mind onto paper and markdown, revisit what matters on a spaced cadence, and let carefully constrained AI act as an apprentice that sharpens your tools — never as a foreman that runs your life.
